hexpl.us

url-based hex lookup

Enter a hex you want to look up in the url, like http://hexpl.us#ffffaa to see the hex's color, RGB value, and decimal value.

Enter a decimal value beginning with a 't,' like http://hexpl.us#t98 to see the hex value.

Enter a color name, like http://hexpl.us#navy to see the color, RGB value, hex value, and decimal value.
